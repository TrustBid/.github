## 🌍 The Problem

Social foundations often lack the infrastructure needed to prove how donor funds are spent.

- Reporting is manual and time-consuming.
- Financial execution is difficult to verify.
- Donors must trust reports instead of verifiable evidence.
- Transparency is limited and auditing can take weeks.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

**TrustBid** transforms every fund disbursement into a verifiable record on the **Stellar blockchain**.

Instead of relying on manual reports, every transaction becomes immutable, transparent, and publicly auditable.

This enables any donor to verify how funds are executed in **seconds**, creating trust through cryptographic proof rather than paperwork.

---

## 🚀 How It Works

1. A foundation creates a project.
2. Donors contribute funds.
3. Every disbursement is recorded on Stellar.
4. Beneficiaries receive funds.
5. Anyone can audit the complete execution history through the blockchain.
